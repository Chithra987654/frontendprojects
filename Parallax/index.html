Kerala Cuisines

Kerala cuisine is a blend of flavors featuring rice, coconut, and spices like pepper, ginger, and cardamom, with seafood being a popular choice due to the state's coastal location. Some of the most well-known dishes include Appam with stew, Puttu and Kadala Curry, and Karimeen Pollichathu. Kerala also offers a variety of breakfast items like Idli, Dosa, Appam, Idiyappam, Puttu, and Pathiri.

Artforms and Festivals

For more than a thousand years, a great many art form have been raised and nurtured in Kerala. From retelling the great epics to exploring the depths of life and its very meaning, we have been witness to the evolution and growth of these performing arts. From Mohiniyattam to Kathakali, our high-resolution visuals cover a variety of art forms.
